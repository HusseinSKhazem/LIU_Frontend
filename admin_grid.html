Users

Administrators have the ability to search and view details for all users in the system, simplifying user management tasks.

Add Users

Administrators hold the authority to both search and add users within the system, streamlining user management tasks and ensuring comprehensive administrative control.

Recruiters

Administrators not only possess the capability to monitor recruiters' activities but also have the authority to approve their actions within the system, ensuring a controlled and secure user environment.

Vacancies

Administrators have the dual responsibility of overseeing recruiters and monitoring available job vacancies, maintaining a comprehensive view of both recruitment activities and job opportunities within the system.

Warnings Log

Administrators have the capability to monitor warning logs, enabling them to track and address any undesirable behaviors or misconduct from students or other users. This feature empowers administrators to maintain a secure and respectful environment within the system.

Login History

Administrators can keep tabs on the login history of all users, providing a valuable tool to ensure security and track user activity within the system.
